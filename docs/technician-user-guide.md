## Logging In

1. Open the Branch360 Google Sheets application
2. Your account is automatically authenticated using your Google account email
3. The system will recognize your role as Technician
4. You'll be taken directly to your personalized dashboard

**Note:** If you don't see your dashboard, contact your administrator to ensure your account is set up correctly.

## Viewing Your Dashboard

Your Technician Dashboard provides an overview of your daily work:

### Today's Route
- **Route ID**: Your assigned route identifier
- **Date**: Current date
- **Total Stops**: Number of stops scheduled
- **Completed Stops**: Stops you've finished
- **Status**: Current route status

**Note:** Route integration with Route1 is coming soon. Currently shows placeholder information.

### Pending Installations
Installations assigned to you that need to be completed:
- **Account Name**: Customer name
- **Service Address**: Installation location
- **Scheduled Date**: When installation is scheduled
- **Service Type**: Type of service to install
- **POC Information**: Point of contact details
- **Special Notes**: Important information about the installation
- **Materials Ordered**: Whether materials have been ordered

### Leads Submitted
Track the leads you've submitted:
- **Lead ID**: Unique identifier
- **Date Submitted**: When you submitted the lead
- **Customer Name**: Business name
- **Status**: Current lead status (New, Contacted, Converted, Lost)
- **Assigned AE**: Which Account Executive received the lead

### Recent Issues
Service issues you've reported or are assigned to:
- **Issue ID**: Unique identifier
- **Date**: When issue was reported
- **Customer**: Customer name
- **Issue Type**: Type of problem
- **Severity**: High, Medium, or Low
- **Status**: Open, In Progress, or Resolved
- **Description**: Details about the issue

## Submitting Leads

When you encounter a potential customer during your route, submit a lead to get them connected with an Account Executive.

### When to Submit a Lead
Submit a lead when:
- A customer asks about services
- You notice a business that might need pest control
- A customer mentions they're unhappy with current provider
- You see signs of pest problems at a location

### How to Submit a Lead

1. Navigate to "Submit Lead" on your dashboard
2. Fill in the required information:
   - **Customer Name**: Business or property owner name (required)
   - **Service Address**: Complete address where service is needed (required)
   - **Zip Code**: 5-digit zip code (required)
   - **Phone**: Customer contact number (optional but recommended)
   - **Email**: Customer email address (optional)
   - **Service Type**: Type of service they're interested in (default: Pest Control)
   - **Notes**: Any relevant information about the lead

3. Click "Submit Lead"
4. The system will:
   - Automatically assign the lead to the correct AE based on zip code territory
   - Generate a unique Lead ID
   - Send a notification to the assigned AE
   - Record your submission for tracking

### Lead Submission Tips
- **Be Complete**: Include as much information as possible
- **Be Accurate**: Double-check addresses and zip codes
- **Be Descriptive**: Add notes about why this is a good lead
- **Be Timely**: Submit leads the same day you discover them
- **Include Contact Info**: Phone numbers help AEs follow up quickly

### What Happens After Submission
1. The lead is immediately assigned to an AE in that territory
2. The AE receives a notification
3. The AE will contact the customer (typically within 4 hours)
4. You can track the lead status in your "Leads Submitted" section

## Viewing Installations

### Pending Installations
View all installations assigned to you:
1. Check the "Pending Installations" section on your dashboard
2. Each installation shows:
   - Customer name and address
   - Scheduled installation date
   - Service type and details
   - Point of contact information
   - Special instructions or notes
   - Whether materials have been ordered

### Installation Details
Click on an installation to see:
- Full customer information
- Service specifications
- Initial job price and maintenance pricing
- Frequency of service
- Any special requirements
- Related notes from the sales rep

### Preparing for Installation
Before your scheduled installation:
1. Review the installation details
2. Confirm materials are ordered
3. Check for special notes or requirements
4. Verify the scheduled date and time
5. Contact the customer if needed to confirm

## Reporting Issues

If you encounter service issues during your route, report them immediately.

### Types of Issues
- **Service Quality**: Problems with service delivery
- **Customer Complaints**: Customer dissatisfaction
- **Equipment Issues**: Problems with equipment or materials
- **Access Issues**: Problems accessing service location
- **Billing Questions**: Customer billing concerns
- **Other**: Any other service-related issues

### How to Report an Issue

1. Navigate to "Report Issue" on your dashboard
2. Fill in the issue details:
   - **Customer Name**: Who the issue affects (required)
   - **Issue Type**: Select from dropdown (required)
   - **Severity**: 
     - **High**: Urgent, needs immediate attention
     - **Medium**: Important, should be addressed soon
     - **Low**: Minor issue, can be handled in normal course
   - **Description**: Detailed explanation of the issue (required)
   - **Assigned Tech**: Leave blank to assign to yourself, or select another tech

3. Click "Submit Issue"
4. The system will:
   - Create an issue record
   - Notify the Operations Manager
   - Assign the issue for tracking
   - Generate an Issue ID

### Issue Status Tracking
Monitor your reported issues:
- **Open**: Issue reported, awaiting action
- **In Progress**: Issue is being worked on
- **Resolved**: Issue has been fixed
- **Escalated**: Issue requires higher-level attention

## Completing Jobs

### Completing an Installation

When you finish an installation:

1. Navigate to the installation in your "Pending Installations" section
2. Click "Complete Installation"
3. Enter completion details:
   - **Completion Date**: Date installation was finished (defaults to today)
   - **Notes**: Any relevant notes about the completion
     - Materials used
     - Issues encountered
     - Special instructions for future service
     - Customer feedback

4. Click "Mark Complete"
5. The system will:
   - Update the installation status to "Complete"
   - Record the completion date
   - Update your installation count
   - Notify the Operations Manager

### Installation Completion Best Practices
- Complete installations immediately after finishing
- Include detailed notes for future reference
- Note any special requirements or customer preferences
- Report any issues encountered during installation
- Confirm customer satisfaction when possible

## Best Practices

### Daily Routine
1. **Start of Day**: Review your dashboard, check pending installations
2. **During Route**: Submit leads as you discover them, report issues immediately
3. **End of Day**: Complete any finished installations, review submitted leads

### Lead Submission
- Submit leads the same day you discover them
- Include complete and accurate information
- Add helpful notes about why it's a good lead
- Follow up on leads you've submitted (check status weekly)

### Issue Reporting
- Report issues immediately when discovered
- Be specific and detailed in descriptions
- Use appropriate severity levels
- Follow up on high-severity issues

### Installation Management
- Review installation details before arrival
- Confirm materials are available
- Complete installations promptly
- Document everything in completion notes

### Communication
- Keep notes detailed and professional
- Include dates and times in notes
- Update status promptly
- Communicate with Operations Manager about issues
